## OddsJam Deep Dive

### What They Are
- Sports betting analytics platform — tools for finding profitable bets
- Scans 100+ sportsbooks in real-time
- Founded by Alex Monahan and Ankit Goyal

### Acquisition
- **Acquired by:** Gambling.com Group
- **Date:** January 1, 2025
- **Total Value:** Up to $160M ($80M upfront: $70M cash + $10M stock, + $80M performance through 2026)
- **Revenue at acquisition:** ~$26M/year
- **EBITDA:** ~$12M
- **Users:** 100,000+

### OddsJam Core Features
1. **+EV (Positive Expected Value) Finder** — flagship tool, finds bets where book odds > true odds
2. **Arbitrage Scanner** — finds risk-free bets between sportsbooks (key user acquisition hook)
3. **Odds Comparison Screen** — real-time odds across 100+ books
4. **Low-Hold Finder** — for converting sportsbook promos/bonuses to cash
5. **Middle Finder** — opportunities to win both sides of a spread
6. **Fantasy Optimizer** — PrizePicks, Underdog Fantasy
7. **Bet Tracker** — log and analyze bets

### OddsJam Pricing
- **Standard:** ~$199/month
- **Platinum:** ~$999/month (more books, faster data, advanced tools)
- 7-day free trial

### OddsJam Marketing Strategy
- **YouTube-first** — hundreds of educational videos explaining arb, +EV, bankroll management
- **Content marketing** — teaching the "why" behind bets built trust
- **Affiliate program** — sports betting creators promoted them
- **Positioning:** "Day trading sports" — treat betting as investment, not entertainment
- **Hook:** Arbitrage = "risk-free profit" was the gateway drug

### OddsJam Weaknesses (User Complaints)
1. **Too expensive** — $199-999/mo hard to justify for many
2. **Data accuracy issues** — phantom lines (odds not actually available), lag in live data
3. **Steep learning curve** — NOT beginner friendly, requires significant time investment
4. **Poor customer service** — slow, unhelpful support
5. **Account limitations** — sportsbooks limit/ban successful users (fundamental business model flaw)
6. **Lawsuit** — Swish Analytics suing for alleged data theft
7. **Conflict of interest allegations** — affiliated company may sell data back to sportsbooks
8. **No AI** — just data and dashboards, user does all the thinking

### OddsJam Competitors
- BetBurger, AVO (Arbs vs Odds), RebelBetting — similar arb/EV tools
- Action Network, BettingPros — more content/picks focused (different market)

- "OddsJam gives you data. We give you an AI betting partner."
- "The first AI-powered sports betting intelligence system"

### Key Differentiators to Lead With
1. **AI-Powered** — not just tools, an intelligent system that thinks for you
2. **Proven Results** — 225-94 (70.5%), +34.7% ROI, 77% high confidence
3. **Dead Simple** — picks delivered to your phone, no dashboards to learn
